A Bowl of Cereal

A nutritious and refreshing bowl of cereal to start your day off right!

This bowl of cereal will energize you like no other meal and get you ready to take on the day. If you are lactose intolerant, DO NOT INCLUDE THE MILK!!!!!

Ingredients

  • Cereal of choice
  • Milk (optional)
  • Sturdy bowl and spoon

Steps

  1. Put the cereal in the bowl
  2. Put the milk in the bowl
  3. Insert the spoon into the bowl
