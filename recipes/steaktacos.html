Steak Tacos

Delicious steak tacos garnished with fresh toppings

This recipe uses only the finest ingredients, make sure to use fresh produce for the best results!

They can also be made less spicy by removing the jalapenos!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Flank Steak
  • Red Onions
  • Avocado
  • Cilantro
  • Jalapeno

Steps

  1. Cook the steak
  2. Cut the red onion and jalapeno
  3. Cut the cilantro and avocado
  4. Assemble the taco
